The story behind Kinedu

Every great product starts with a personal question. Mine was simple.

The question

Like many parents, I often wondered: "Am I doing enough to support my children's development?" With four amazing daughters, that question stayed with me every day. That's why I built Kinedu — a resource I wish I had from the very beginning of my parenting journey.

Advenio — where it began

Before Kinedu, I co-founded Advenio, the first company to build and operate corporate-sponsored early learning centers across Latin America. We grew to 26 centers in Mexico and Colombia — and I saw firsthand how parents felt dropping off their kids: a mix of guilt, pride, and a deep desire to be more involved.

Building Kinedu

That's what inspired me to create something different — a proactive toolkit in the palm of parents' hands, feeding them information, ideas, knowledge, and tools to play with their babies and truly make a difference.

Becoming a dad

In 2016, when I became a parent myself, everything became even more real. Every laugh, every milestone, every sleepless night reminded me why this work matters so much.
