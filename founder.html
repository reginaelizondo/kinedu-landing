Welcome to Kinedu

With four daughters, one question never left me: "Am I doing enough?" Before Kinedu, I co-founded Advenio — 26 early learning centers across Mexico and Colombia. I saw firsthand how much parents wanted to be involved in their child's development.

That inspired Kinedu — a toolkit that puts expert guidance directly in parents' hands. When I became a dad in 2016, every laugh and milestone reminded me why this work matters so much.
