Today it is increasingly common for parents to seek advice on the upbringing and care of their little ones. For this reason, various companies have created apps to track your baby’s development from different approaches.

In this article, we will talk about 3 apps that you can download to track your little one’s development.

1. Baby Tracker

This app allows you to record important information about your little one’s daily life, for example:

  • The times they eat and the amount and type of food they consume (breast milk, formula, or solids)
  • Number of diaper changes
  • Sleep scheme
  • Growth
  • Developmental milestones

The system also allows you to add notes and photos of your little one. This can be of great help when you go for a check-up with the pediatrician and can show them the evolution of any physical symptoms, for example, a skin rash.

Another advantage of the application is that it creates an automatic backup of all the information you add about your baby in iCloud or Dropbox and it is synchronized on all the electronic devices that you authorize. This way, all of your child’s caregivers can access the records and add data.

2. Tiny beans

From the moment they’re born, your little one will surprise you and give you many moments of joy. Therefore, this app is an excellent option to document the growth of your child.

This software creates photo albums that make it easy for you to have and organize all your little one’s pictures in one place, in addition to protecting them from any eventuality. In turn, it allows you to edit photos of your child and share them with your friends and family.

3. Kinedu (the best app to track your baby’s development!)

This app has information about the development of children from birth to 6 years old. When you start using it, Kinedu will ask you to answer some questions to make an initial assessment of your baby’s development.

Then, you will get a science-based activity plan that is completely personalized to your child’s age and needs to encourage their development through play.

This tool will show you the progress of your little one so that you can track their development with progress reports in each area: physical, linguistic, cognitive, and social and emotional. The system is based on the developmental milestones of the American Academy of Pediatrics (AAP) and the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC).

Although each child grows at a different pace, having a guide can help you stimulate your little one’s skills at each stage and follow their progress with the help of their pediatrician.

Another advantage of this app for parents is that it can be shared with family members and other caregivers so that they can access your child’s activity plan and monitor their development while playing and spending quality time with them.

Since its launch, Kinedu has been used by 7 million families around the world — you can download it for free here.

Now you know more about 3 apps to track your baby’s development with which you can follow the growth of your little one.
